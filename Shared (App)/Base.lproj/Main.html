You can turn on FFZ for Safari’s extension in Safari Extensions preferences. Follow these steps:

  1. Go to extensions settings in Safari
  2. Enable FFZ for Safari by ticking the checkmark
  3. In Safari settings, click on Websites
  4. Scroll down to FFZ for Twitch and check allow for Twitch
